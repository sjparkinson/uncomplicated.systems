# Coffee at the Troubadour

Stories from my mothers time as a London estate agent.

_People and company names have been changed to protect their identities, assuming they were even real in the first place..._

24th May 2008

Saturday morning. Appointment to show flats … at 11am.

Greg Philips came into the office at 10:30am. He asked about a flat at £1m and another at £1.6m, he said he was buying it for his grandchild. I gave him details to take away and put all his information into the computer.

As I was going on a viewing this morning I said that he should come back at 1pm.

He was back at the office on time, and I took him in my car to see Simon's flat at £1.6m.

Greg said that he wanted to make sure that his grandchild was taken care of, that he had a 30 year old daughter who was married and independent.

He was divorced.
